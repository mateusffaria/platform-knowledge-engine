# Senior Platform Engineer

Northstar Systems is building dependable developer infrastructure for teams that ship local-first products.

## Responsibilities

- Design and operate reliable platform services for internal engineering teams.
- Collaborate with product engineers to improve developer workflows and deployment safety.
- Lead technical design discussions and document operational decisions.

## Requirements

- 5+ years of experience building backend or platform systems.
- TypeScript, Node.js, and PostgreSQL experience.
- Experience with Docker and Kubernetes in production environments.
- Strong communication and stakeholder management skills.
- Comfort working across ambiguous product areas.
- Experience in SaaS or developer-tooling domains.

## Preferred Qualifications

- React experience for internal platform interfaces.
- Terraform and AWS experience.
- English fluency for distributed collaboration.
